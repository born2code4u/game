1.1 General

{{player.subsession.session.vars.selfremarket.generaldescription}}
Regions are separeted by flexible Boundaries , which may be changed after each session.

Each region is related to a sales contract type. The Buyer of a contract will have to pay P+|X-C|. P is the base price, he negotiated with seller. X is the destination he decides to get the commodity to* and C is called center of the region. X has to be inside the region. The Seller has to either bring the commodity himself or buy it and let someone else bring it.

Thus a consumer should purchase the contract for the region in which he consumes. But a producer can transport to all sites and likely has to be willing to sell his material into all regions.

If players are unballanced after the last negotiation round, they will have to export or import. Price for import is 100 and for export 0 per commodity unit.

Sales and purchases will be evaluated against a Benchmark Price.
